What is LASA 'n Found?

LASA 'n Found is a crowdsourced solution to LASA's lost and found problem. You can either browse for a lost item, or submit a new one to our database.

How it Works

Our homepage shows the 10 most recently submitted items in our database, to look further back in time, our archive function lets you look through all current Lost 'n Found items. An item in the database expires after 2 weeks, meaning if it isn't claimed, the item is deleted. To add an item that you have found, please go to the Report a New Lost Item page. To claim an item, make sure it is actually yours, complete a Captcha, and hit 'Claim Me!'.
